Essential Workers Deserve Better

2023 NSW State Election Campaign Brand.
This campaign was inspired by "It's Time"-era hopefulness and political optimism, centering workers as key players in a message designed to motivate. Deliverables included logos, logotypes, print materials (flyers, petitions, corflutes, selfie signs), campaign images, merchandise, social media content.
